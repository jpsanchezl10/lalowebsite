The Artist

Lalo Sánchez del Valle, born in Mexico City in 1968, is a visionary abstract artist whose work transcends conventional boundaries. His creations are a testament to the power of subconscious expression, each piece a window into the depths of human emotion and experience.

Sánchez del Valle's artistic process begins with the purest form of abstraction, oxidizing colors on canvas to create an inconstant marbling effect with perfectly accidental transparency. From this foundation emerges an internal struggle, a reflection of the complexities we all face in navigating society.

The artist then employs ink, acrylics, and pastels to reaffirm with color the dreams that the inner child has liberated, resulting in unique and emotionally charged works that resonate with viewers on a profound level.
